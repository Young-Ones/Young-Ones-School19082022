About

Who we are ?

Young Ones School is an online learning platform for children to learn extracurricular activities from age of 4 to 14. We are providing courses in Art & Craft, Spoken English & Public Speaking, Yoga through animations and live sessions.

Learning extracurricular activities helps children's holistic development by making them realize their potential, improving academic performance and professional development. We focus on an active, experience-based, and creative approach to learning.

Young Ones was inaugurated by Padma Shri Dr.Mylswamy Annadurai (Indian Scientist, Moon Man Of India). Young Ones has trained over 12,000+ students with the help of 100+ educators.

“Change is the end result of all true learning”

What do we do?

We work with schools to provide our empowering and engaging programs to all children with quality at an affordable cost.

Our Vision and Mission:

Our vision is to make children understand the true virtue of education and knowledge making them to explore this universe and themselves in a fabulous way thereby revealing their true potential.

Providing Quality education with affordable cost is our mission.We are building it and working with schools to make this possible.

How are we different ?

We just don't teach academics to children we teach them Learn how to Learn. We do this with Certified trainers who offers quality education and quality teachings. We make children happy by educating them.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register with Young Ones School?

    Thank you for making the right decision for a better future. To register with Young Ones School, please click on the WhatsApp link on our website, which will direct you to us. We are happy to assist you!

  • Do you have a ‘Quality Curriculum’?

    The curriculum of Young Ones School is planned and structured by certified professionals and educators.

  • Do you provide live classes or pre-recorded one?

    Young Ones School offers both online and offline programs. On our online platform, we provide live classes. Don't worry if you missed a session. You can utilize the recorded videos and class materials anytime in the app.

  • In what way Young Ones School is different from other platforms?

    Young Ones School educators are fluent in English as well as regional languages, which is beneficial to learners, and Young Ones School offers excellent programs at a reasonable cost. We provide online and offline materials as per the programs.

  • Does Young Ones School provide certificates after the course/Internship completion?

    Yes, the enrolled student must complete the course/internship, to receive the E-Certificate.

  • Do you provide assistance after completion of the class too?

    Our team is here to provide you with information and support at any time.

  • What is the language of instruction-English or regional language?

    Instructions are given in both English and regional languages. Further, we examine the learner’s demands and we teach accordingly to enhance them.

  • Do you have a dashboard to keep track of learner’s progress?

    Absolutely yes! You can track your progress and we are always connected with you for progress-related queries.

  • How many courses can a learner sign in ?

    There are no limitations. Please contact our team before enrolling to check that the sessions you need do not fall at the same time.

  • Do I have the option to transfer membership to another person?

    This option is available before the commencement of the course. Once the classes start, this option becomes invalid.

  • Is your portal safe to join?

    Learners, Your information is in safe hands.

  • Would I get a refund, if I didn’t attend the class?

    A refund is available if the registration is canceled within 7 days of enrollment.

  • What are the payment methods?

    Payments can be made via the internet. To pay the cost, you can use UPI, wallet, or net banking.

  • Can I manage my studies along with Extracurricular?

    You can. Time management skill plays an important role here. It is a platform to exhibit your interests and exercise your skills with peers, you can grasp, learn and do things quickly. Young Ones School extracurricular classes are scheduled to be held in the evening and weekend.
